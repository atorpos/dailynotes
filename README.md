# 📝 DailyNotes

> *Because my brain has the memory capacity of a goldfish on vacation* 🐠

## What Is This?

This is my personal digital brain dump—a place where thoughts go to live instead of vanishing into the void at 3 AM.  Think of it as a external hard drive for my consciousness, minus the USB cable and compatibility issues.

## Why Does This Exist?

Ever had a brilliant idea in the shower, only to forget it by the time you grab a towel? Yeah, me too. About 47 times a day. 

This repository is my defense mechanism against: 
- 🧠 Brain fog
- 💭 Shower thoughts that dissolve faster than soap
- 🎯 "I'll definitely remember this" (Narrator:  *They did not remember*)
- 📱 Phone notes that get lost in a sea of shopping lists

## What's Inside? 

- **miscnotes.md** - The junk drawer of my mind.  Random thoughts, half-baked ideas, and things that seemed important at the time (they probably weren't).

# 📝 每日筆記 (DailyNotes)

> *因為我的大腦記憶容量跟度假中的金魚差不多* 🐠

## 這是什麼？

這是我的個人數位大腦垃圾場——一個讓想法好好活著的地方，而不是在凌晨三點消失在虛空中。把它想像成我意識的外接硬碟，但不需要 USB 線，也沒有相容性問題。

## 為什麼會有這個？

你有過在洗澡時想到絕妙點子，結果拿毛巾時就忘記的經驗嗎？對，我也有。一天大概 47 次。

這個倉庫是我對抗以下情況的防禦機制：
- 🧠 腦霧
- 💭 洗澡時的靈感，比肥皂泡泡消失得還快
- 🎯 「我絕對會記得」（旁白：*他們並沒有記得*）
- 📱 手機備忘錄，淹沒在購物清單的汪洋中

## 裡面有什麼？

- **miscnotes.md** - 我大腦的雜物抽屜。隨機想法、半生不熟的點子，還有當時覺得很重要的東西（大概都不重要）。
