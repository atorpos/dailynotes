Phase 1: Define scope and involve key stakeholders to set boundaries before engaging with databases and cloud environments. Draft a standard PIA questionnaire for system owners to complete. 

Phase 2: Conduct a technical data inventory, map data flows, and identify undocumented or legacy data outside governance.

Phase 3: Categorize data into tiers, assess current controls, and identify compliance gaps.

Phase 4: Develop governance policies, standardize access, and plan data consolidation based on mapping.

Phase 5: Automate governance enforcement through CI/CD, automate data pruning, and schedule regular reviews.
